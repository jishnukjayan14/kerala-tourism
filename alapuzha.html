INTRODUCTION

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is a picturesque district nestled in the southern Indian state of Kerala, celebrated for its enchanting backwaters and serene landscapes. Renowned as the "Venice of the East," Alappuzha is a key player in Kerala's vibrant tourism scene. The district is a haven for nature enthusiasts and cultural aficionados alike, offering a unique blend of tranquility and vibrant local life. The mesmerizing backwaters, adorned with traditional houseboats, showcase a distinctive way of life, providing an unforgettable experience for visitors. Alappuzha is also famed for its annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race, a spectacular event that attracts thousands of spectators. With its charming canals, lush greenery, and cultural richness, Alappuzha stands as a testament to Kerala's allure as a premier tourist destination.
