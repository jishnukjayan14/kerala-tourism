INTRODUCTION

Nestled in the picturesque state of Kerala, Kasaragod district stands as a hidden gem awaiting discovery by avid travelers. Renowned for its pristine beaches, historic forts, and cultural richness, Kasaragod is a captivating destination for those seeking an off-the-beaten-path adventure. The district's long shoreline is adorned with golden sands, with iconic destinations such as Bekal Fort providing a perfect blend of history and scenic beauty. The unique cultural tapestry of Kasaragod, influenced by diverse communities, adds a vibrant touch to the region, making it a melting pot of traditions. Visitors can explore the enchanting backwaters, witness traditional Theyyam performances, and indulge in the authentic flavors of Malabar cuisine. With its untouched natural beauty and rich cultural heritage, Kasaragod promises an immersive and unforgettable experience for those seeking a tranquil escape in the heart of God's Own Country.
