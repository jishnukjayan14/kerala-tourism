INTRODUCTION

Pathanamthitta district, nestled in the southern state of Kerala, India, is a captivating destination that seamlessly blends natural beauty with cultural richness. Renowned for its lush landscapes, pristine rivers, and verdant hills, Pathanamthitta is a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. The district boasts the picturesque Periyar National Park, a biodiversity hotspot that attracts wildlife enthusiasts and trekkers. Pilgrimage also takes center stage in Pathanamthitta, as the district is home to the famous Sabarimala Temple, drawing millions of devotees annually. The tranquil backwaters of the Pamba River offer a serene retreat, allowing visitors to unwind amidst the soothing rhythm of nature. Pathanamthitta stands as a testament to Kerala's diversity, offering a harmonious blend of spirituality and natural splendor, making it an ideal destination for those seeking a unique and enriching tourism experience.
